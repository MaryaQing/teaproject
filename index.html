Green Tea:
Green tea is known for its numerous health benefits. It’s rich in antioxidants and can help improve brain function, increase fat burning, and lower the risk of certain types of cancer. Its refreshing, light flavor makes it a popular choice for many tea lovers.

Herbal Tea:
Herbal teas are made from a variety of plants, flowers, and herbs, such as chamomile, peppermint, and lavender. They are caffeine-free and often used for their calming and soothing effects, making them perfect for relaxing after a long day.

Oolong Tea:
Oolong tea is partially fermented, giving it a flavor profile that’s somewhere between green and black tea. It’s known for its floral and fruity taste and is packed with antioxidants that promote heart health, aid in digestion, and boost metabolism.

Black Tea:
Black tea is a strong, robust tea with a deep flavor. It’s the most widely consumed tea in the world and comes in many varieties like Earl Grey, Darjeeling, and Assam. It contains more caffeine than other types of tea, making it a great morning pick-me-up.
